About RNews

What is RNews?

RNews is a community-driven aggregator for scientific papers and links, built for researchers who want a fast, minimalist way to discover, share, and discuss the literature that matters.

Submitting items

Any registered user can submit a link or a scientific paper. For papers, paste the URL (DOI, arXiv, PubMed, or any journal page) and click Fetch metadata — the system will try to automatically fill in the journal, authors, and publication date using CrossRef, arXiv, PubMed, and optionally a local Zotero translation server.

Tags

Every item requires at least one tag. Tags are lowercase, shared across all users, and color-coded for quick scanning. You can save tags to your profile and use them to filter your personal page or team pages.

Teams

Teams are private or public spaces where groups of researchers can curate their own feed. Items submitted directly to a team are team-only; items shared from the main feed keep their public presence while also appearing in the team. Roles: admin, contributor, viewer.

Voting & comments

Upvote items and comments. Click a title to open the comment thread, where replies are nested Reddit-style and sorted by score.

Search & permalinks

The search bar at the top supports full-text title search. Toggle by tag to find items matching specific tags — the resulting URL is a stable permalink you can bookmark or share.
